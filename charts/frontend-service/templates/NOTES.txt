Thank you for installing the frontend!

Your UI is now deployed and accessible through Traefik Ingress.

To check the Deployment:
  kubectl get deploy {{ include "frontend.fullname" . }}

To check the Pods:
  kubectl get pods -l app={{ include "frontend.name" . }}

To check the Service:
  kubectl get svc {{ include "frontend.fullname" . }}

To verify Traefik routing:
  kubectl get ingress {{ include "frontend.fullname" . }}

Once deployed successfully, access the frontend at:
  http://{{ .Values.ingress.host }}/
